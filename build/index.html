Introducing ARISE Network

ARISE (Africa Research, Implementation Science and Education) Network

The ARISE Network is a network for collaborative education and research activities in Africa. ARISE comprises health experts and institutions from Harvard University, the Africa Academy of Public Health in Tanzania, Botswana, Burkina Faso, Ethiopia, Ghana, Nigeria, South Africa, and Uganda who share similar ambitions.

Objectives and Research Tasks


Objective 1: Determine the key health risk factors and intervention needs among adolescents in SSA.
Objective 2: Develop a methodological framework for researching adolescent health interventions in SSA.
Objective 3: Locally design and pilot test adolescent health literacy and nudge interventions.
Objective 4: Establish effects, impacts, and mechanisms of adolescent health literacy and nudge interventions on health knowledge and understanding, health behaviors, and outcomes in SSA.
Objective 5: Establish effects, impacts, and mechanisms of key national and regional adolescent health policies in SSA.
